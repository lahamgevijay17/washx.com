# WashX - Professional Doorstep Car Wash Service

**Xtra Clean. Xtra Fast.**

> A modern, responsive website for WashX - India's premium doorstep car wash service that brings professional car care directly to your location.

## 🚗 About WashX

WashX is a professional car wash service that revolutionizes vehicle maintenance by bringing high-quality cleaning services directly to customers' doorsteps. Founded with a mission to save time while delivering exceptional results, WashX serves customers across major Indian cities including Mumbai, Pune, and Bangalore.

### 🌟 Core Values
- **Convenience**: No more waiting in queues or driving to car wash centers
- **Quality**: Professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly products
- **Reliability**: Trained experts with 100% satisfaction guarantee
- **Affordability**: Premium service at competitive pricing starting from ₹199

## 🎯 Business Model

### 💼 Service Packages
1. **Basic Exterior Wash** - ₹199 (30-40 mins)
   - Exterior foam wash, glass cleaning, tire shine

2. **Full Interior + Exterior** - ₹299 (45-60 mins) *Most Popular*
   - Everything in Basic + interior vacuum, dashboard polish, seat cleaning

3. **Premium Detailing** - ₹599 (60-90 mins)
   - Everything in Full + wax protection, engine bay cleaning, air freshener

4. **Monthly Packages** - ₹1299/month
   - 4 full services + priority booking + free emergency wash

## 🔧 Installation & Usage

1. **Clone the repository**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/washx-website.git
   cd washx-website
   ```

2. **Open in browser**
   - Simply open `index.html` in your web browser
   - No build process required - pure HTML/CSS/JS

3. **Customization**
   - Update contact details in all HTML files
   - Replace placeholder images with actual photos
   - Modify service packages and pricing as needed
   - Update service areas and coverage locations
