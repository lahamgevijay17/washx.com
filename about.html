About WashX

Your trusted partner for professional doorstep car wash services

Our Story

Founded with a mission to revolutionize car care, WashX brings professional car wash services directly to your doorstep. We understand that your time is valuable, and your car deserves the best care possible.

Our team of trained professionals uses premium, eco-friendly products and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your vehicle receives the care it deserves. We're not just washing cars – we're providing convenience, quality, and peace of mind.

With thousands of satisfied customers across multiple cities, WashX has become synonymous with reliability, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide convenient, eco-friendly, and professional car wash services that save our customers time while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and preferred doorstep car care service, expanding to every city and making car maintenance effortless for everyone.
